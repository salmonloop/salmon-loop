## 2025-04-26 - Adding Structural Focus Indicators in Terminal Lists
**Learning:** In Ink-based terminal UIs, relying solely on text color (e.g., `green` vs `gray`) to indicate the currently selected item in a list creates accessibility issues for colorblind users and in terminal environments where color support is limited or disabled.
**Action:** Always include a structural or character-based focus indicator (such as `❯ ` or `> ` vs `  `) alongside any color changes for list item focus states to ensure universal accessibility.
