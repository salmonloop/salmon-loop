## 2024-05-18 - Consistent Focus Indicators
**Learning:** For list selections in terminal UIs, relying solely on color changes or subtle structural elements like `│ ` can be insufficient for accessibility, especially for colorblind users. A clear, explicit character-based focus indicator like `❯ ` improves discoverability and consistency.
**Action:** Always include a structural or character-based focus indicator (specifically `❯ ` for consistency) rather than relying solely on color changes.
