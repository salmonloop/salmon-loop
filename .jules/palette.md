## 2025-05-27 - Consistent Focus Indicators in Ink UI
**Learning:** Relying on ambiguous characters (`│`) or solely color changes for selection lists in Ink terminal UIs reduces accessibility for colorblind users and causes inconsistency.
**Action:** Always include a structural or character-based focus indicator (specifically `❯ ` for consistency) for list selections, ensuring spacing (`'  '`) is preserved when unselected to prevent layout shifting.
