## 2024-05-20 - Structural Focus Indicator for CommandSuggestionList
**Learning:** For list selections in Ink terminal UI components, relying solely on color changes and simple vertical bars ('│ ') is insufficient for accessibility, especially for colorblind users. Structural or character-based focus indicators provide clearer navigation hints.
**Action:** Always include a distinct structural or character-based focus indicator (specifically `❯ ` for consistency) rather than relying solely on color changes, to ensure accessibility for colorblind users.
