## 2024-05-16 - Consistent Structural Focus Indicator
**Learning:** Replacing color-only or ambiguous selection indicators (like `│ `) with explicit structural characters (like `❯ `) greatly improves accessibility for colorblind users and creates a consistent interactive pattern across list-based CLI components.
**Action:** Use `❯ ` consistently for list selection focus states in all Ink terminal UI components rather than relying on color or plain bars.
