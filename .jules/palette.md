## 2024-05-13 - Focus Indicators in Ink
**Learning:** For list selections in Ink terminal UI components, relying solely on color changes can be problematic for colorblind users. It is important to include a structural or character-based focus indicator (e.g., `❯ `).
**Action:** Always include a visual, structural indicator for currently selected items in terminal UIs (like `❯ ` for selected, and `  ` for unselected), in addition to any color highlighting.
